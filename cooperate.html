Corporate Social Responsibility

CIB plays an important role in society and has a responsibility to ensure that our employees and communities are cared for and are given equal opportunity to develop and flourish as a result of being part of CIB. We make a difference by helping people to operate in a safe and secure environment where they can thrive and prosper.

We recognize that the sustainable growth of our business depends on our unwavering commitment to good corporate governance, the development and engagement of our people, proactively identifying and mitigating risks, and the sound partnerships that we build with our customers and our local communities.

Corporate social responsibility is therefore an integral part of our everyday work.

In order to support Government development initiatives, CIB shall focus its assistance to four main areas of health, education, social welfare and disasters. We take our responsibility to "A Stable and Systematic Caring Organization" very seriously.
