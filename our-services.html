Our Roles

In short our role is that of professional adviser.

  • Advise on the best Insurance Company
  • Advice your Company on insurance matters and the covers best suited to your needs

  • Provide continuing and independent advice on loss prevention and Administration
  • Assist /control Insurance programs with minimum cost

  • Advise your Company of your rights and obligations
  • Arrange completion of claim forms at time of loss
