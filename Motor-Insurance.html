Our Roles

In short our role is that of professional adviser.

We shall therefore:-

  • - Advise on the best Insurance Company;
  • - Advice your Company on insurance matters and the covers best suited to your needs;
  • - Negotiate for best terms of cover on your behalf;
  • - Provide continuing and independent advice on loss prevention and Administration;
  • - Assist /control yourself Insurance programs, where necessary, with the Minimum cost practicable;
  • - Assist with claim processing by way of:
    • (I) Immediate notification of losses to Insurers
    • (ii) Advise your Company of your rights and obligation under the policy.
    • (iii) Arrange for the completion of appropriate claim forms at time of loss.

NB: We charge no fees for services rendered to our client.
